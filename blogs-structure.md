All blog articles should roughly follow a similar structure:

- Catchy title
Intro:
- Introduce the general topic and why it is relevant today
- Introduce the dilemma or the point of debate
- Present some popular arguments or theories
- Situate and present my argument

Body:
- Develop first point in argument
- Develop second point in argument
- Develop third point in argument

Conclusion:
- Summarise the key points
- Present my final thoughts and reflections


Please be sure to find relevant sources to include to situate the arguments in discussions happening today. Refer to articles from reliable sources like the FT, Economist, The Times, The Telegraph - not just the Guardian which is not a serious paper and extremely left-wing. Also refer to unconventional sources like podcasts - the Rest is Politics, Joe Rogan, A Cheeky Pint, Dwarkesh Podcast. . Dragonomics. Oxford China Policy Lab.
